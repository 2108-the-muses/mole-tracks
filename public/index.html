Mole Tracks

An app to help you monitor changes in your moles*

*

No, not those moles

Skin cancer is the most diagnosed cancer in America, but if caught early, has a 99% survival rate.

Using Mole Tracks, users can easily look out for developing warning signs that a mole might need medical attention. Users can create a mole and plot it on a body map. Then, they are able to create an entry for that mole with a photo, notes, and tags. The compare entries feature allows users to compare two entries side by side. Our app also includes an experimental machine learning feature that, once fully operational, will use AI to help warn users of moles that might be malignant.
